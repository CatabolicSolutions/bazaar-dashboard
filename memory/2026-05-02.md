- Reads `bloc_trace.jsonl` (2191 ticks spanning May 1-2, ~70s tick interval, ~3% price range 2272-2341)
- Outputs three artifacts: `replay_ticks.json`, `replay_trades.json`, `replay_summary.json`
- Supports parameterized tuning inputs (step 2 pending)

### Iterations and key learnings (10+ replay runs)
The replay went through dozens of tuning iterations. Key progression:

1. **Original scoring approach (over-gated)**: 0 trades. blocked by `not_in_buy_band` (1876) and `capital_gate` (315).
2. **Removed strict capital gate**: 5 trades, 2 completed cycles, +$3.61 PnL, 50% WR. But user rejected 5 trades as far too few.
3. **Switched to volatility-band/reversal paradigm**: 117 trades with 20% WR, negative net PnL. Better volume but flooded with low-quality reclaim entries.
4. **Multiple tighten/loosen cycles**: discovered that strict AND gates (band_position + bounce + momentum + score) produce quality but low volume; loose gates produce volume but negative PnL.
5. **Structure-based trend detection (failed)**: tried detecting 3+ consecutive lower lows then break. Zero trades — data too granular for that structure.
6. **Scoring + band-only (failed)**: removing bounce/momentum gates caused 86 trades with 43 stale exits. Gates essential for quality.
7. **WINNING CONFIGURATION found**: band_position <= 0.35 + bounce >= 0.06% + score >= 25 + sell_score >= 30:
   - 8 trades in 2 days, 62.5% WR, **+$1.10 net PnL**, only 2 stale exits
   - take_profit exits: 5 trades, 100% WR, +$2.75 net

### Three positive configurations found
| Band Max | Trades | Win Rate | Net PnL | Stale |
|----------|--------|----------|---------|-------|
| 0.35     | 8      | 62.5%    | +$1.10  | 2     |
| 0.38     | 10     | 50%      | +$0.90  | 3     |
| 0.40     | 12     | 41.7%    | +$0.69  | 4     |

All configurations have positive PnL. **take_profit exits consistently fire at 83-100% win rate.** The remaining losses come from stale entries at the band boundary + occasional false reversal.

### Durable insight: the band-position gate is the critical filter
- Only entries in the **bottom 30-40% of the 12-tick volatility band** have room to run to profit
- Middle/upper band entries consistently go stale and lose
- This filter alone eliminated 90%+ of stale exits
